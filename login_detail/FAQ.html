FAQ
    

 Why would this application occur?

According to the statistics from Census and Statistics Department, there were approximately 36 millions people who had travelled from January to August in 2018. There is no doubt that travelers always wait for luggage at the carousel. It might take hours sometimes if any incident happens.

In this project, we are going to create a luggage shipping website, which can ship the luggage to travelers’ hotel to make good use of the time. User can pay for the shipping fee and track their luggage on the website. If the luggage is lost or delayed, airline will use blockchain to keep updated. Therefore, that updated information can be a proven document to make an insurance claim. User no longer needs to wait for luggage and request a document issued by airline at the airport.

We aim at creating an online platform which provides a safety transaction and shipping services to users so that they can spend their time on something meaningful like exploring the city.

 Who are suitable to use this service?

#luggage is targeting people who travel around the world. There are several reasons. Firstly, people travel around the world may get exhausted immediately after travelling in the airplane a long time. By using our app, they can do whatever they want without carrying their luggage around. For example, tourists can go to the first tour directly and no need to go to hotel to put down their luggage first. It is more time-efficient for a traveller to enjoy more time in their journey.

Secondly, travellers may afraid of troublesome in their trip. Our application can provide efficient way for them to claim luggage assurance as well as checking the status of their luggage. It is believed that travellers no need to worry about their luggage and the quality of trips will be enhanced.

 What are the systems?

Luggage Navigation System

The system equipped with the sensor which examine the status of each luggage.Creators could search and get the update information of their luggage in blockchain in order to check its status including damaged luggage and delay incidents ,and its location.

Luggage Logistics System

After arrived at the destination, creators could request the logistics system for delivering their luggages to their hotel or specific destination. The logistics system would describe the real-time traffic condition and weather reports to all of the user.

Compensation System

The insurance agent would determine the compensation by receiving the recent information of the luggage delay or unexpected incidents. The luggage data of navigation system and logistics system would be the provement of the compensation , which could guarantee both parties’s property.

Payment System

User could pay for the transportation fees or claim the compensation fees through the payment system which supports credit card and bitcoins.

 If my luggage cannot be successfully delivered, what can I do?

You can contact us through email or phone. We will help you to check the transportation details of the luggage. If it is found that the luggage is lost, you can claim the insurance via our website.

 What is SPAGHETTI RUPUTATION?

SPAGHETTI RUPUTATION indicates the credibility. If your SPAGHETTI RUPUTATION is too low, it will affect the amount of Logistic/Insuarnce plans you can use. The SPAGHETTI RUPUTATION will be deducted when you violent the rules or abuse of our services. For example, if you use our service to do illegal activity, your reputation will be duducted or your account will even be blocked.

 How can I increase my SPAGHETTI RUPUTATION?

Your SPAGHETTI RUPUTATION will be increased a little bit after completing each service. But if you are in full reupation(i.e.10/10), your reputation will not be increased.
