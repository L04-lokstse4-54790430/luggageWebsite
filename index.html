Spaghetti Blockchain Application

Spaghetti #luggage provides a safety transaction and shipping services to travellers. It can track for clients’ luggage routine and automatically generate compensation invoice through smart contract. Travellers need not to worry about their luggage. Fully enjoy their trips!

Kickstart Video

Spaghetti Luggage Navigation System

The system equipped with the sensor which examine the status of each luggage.Creators could search and get the update information of their luggage in blockchain in order to check its status including damaged luggage and delay incidents ,and its location.

Spaghetti Luggage Logistics

After arrived at the destination, creators could request the logistics system for delivering their luggages to their hotel or specific destination. The logistics system would describe the real-time traffic condition and weather reports to all of the user.

Spaghetti Compensation

The spaghetti insurance agent would determine the compensation by receiving the recent information of the luggage delay or unexpected incidents. The luggage data of navigation system and logistics system would be the provement of the compensation , which could guarantee both parties’s property.
